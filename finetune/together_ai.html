Fine-tuning small LLMs for transcript formatting

A short report on what we learned.

Raw speech-to-text output is unreadable: no punctuation, no capitalization, numbers spelled out. The job: turn it into something a person wants to read.

We finetuned five open models (270M–9B) on this task and compared them to their unfinetuned bases.

Headline: the best finetune gets formatting right 92% of the time, with typical responses under half a second.
